Media Production

Throughout the last decade of my life, I've been involved in all kinds of media production from live sound to video switching to video producing and editing. Some of this has been done in church environments, while other parts of it has been done with my work at 12th Man Productions at Texas A&M University. Below I will try to encompass any projects I have worked on publically, but as most of my work over the years hasn't been the type I've been able to record or have rights to, I'm limited to the kinds of things I can showcase.

Audio Engineering

Countdown to Kickoff | Johnny Manziel 11.7.15

Audio operator for Countdown to Kickoff from 11.7.15 interview with Heisman Trophy winner and then Cleveland Browns quarterback Johnny Manziel at 12th Man Studios prior to the Texas A&M/Auburn football game. (aired 11/7/15, Kyle Field)

Countdown to Kickoff | Von Miller 9.19.15

Audio operator for Countdown to Kickoff from 9.19.15 interview with Von Miller, Texas A&M All-American and member of the Denver Broncos, prior to the Texas A&M/Nevada football game. Miller later became Super Bowl 50 MVP when his team played in Super Bowl 50 following that regular season. (aired 9/19/15, Kyle Field)
