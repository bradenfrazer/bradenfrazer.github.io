Risky Ribbit

Risky Ribbit is an Android game developed in Unity with a team for my Senior capstone project at Texas A&M. The application is being developed for two professors in the Economics department as a means of studying the economic principles of risk aversion and time-based consumption in children ages 5-16. For the project, I designed all of the UI, graphical assets, and menus used in the game. The game is currently in progress so the screenshots shown above are tentative and subject to change, but are real functioning assets and not mockups.
