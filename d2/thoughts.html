Audio Mixing

Audio mixing is something that is physically done on a sound console, taking audio sources and giving them certain volume levels to create an overall mix or "feel" to the sound. It is a great visual representation of how some people like to hear some things more than others (instruments vs. vocals), which is why I have made it my metaphor. It also presents a new idea that people act as audio mixers every day, tuning out some audio (student ignoring professor's lecture) and tuning in others (trying to listen to music in a crowded room)
